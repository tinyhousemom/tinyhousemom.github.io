About

I am a stay-at-home mom and currently homeschooling my 5 year old son.  My Husband and I have felt led for a while to downsize our possessions and house to simplify and be more focused on what God would have us to do. The journey at this time has us in the middle of purging our possessions as well as planning the layout of our Tiny House on Wheels. It is not an easy journey but such a rewarding one. We have been so blessed every step of the way. We wanted to start this blog to document and share our journey.  We pray that our story inspires and encourages you to search God's will for your life even if He leads you outside the norm of society. Feel free to comment or send any questions via Facebook or the Chat option on the Blog. Stay tuned for more blogs.
